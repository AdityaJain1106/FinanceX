Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is FinanceX?
FinanceX is an all-in-one platform designed to simplify finance for everyone. It offers educational content, real-time analytics, comparison tools, calculators, and personalized insights to help you make smarter financial decisions.
Is FinanceX free to use?
Yes, most features on FinanceX are free to access. Some advanced tools and insights may require sign-in or premium access in the future.
What can I learn in LearnZone?
LearnZone offers deep insights into financial terminology and basic concepts. It provides a left-side term and right-side explanation model for better understanding, suitable for beginners and finance enthusiasts alike.
What is LiveAnalytics?
LiveAnalytics is a module that brings you real-time financial data, trends, and visual insights sourced from multiple APIs like GNews, NewsAPI, and Bing News (Microsoft Azure).
How does DecideX help in financial decision-making?
DecideX provides detailed comparison cards for various banks, including information about loans, fees, interest rates, credit cards, and more—making decision-making clear and transparent.
What calculators are available in SmartCal-C?
SmartCal-C features calculators for EMI, Retirement, Fixed Deposits (FD), Public Provident Fund (PPF), and many more to help you simulate and plan your financial journey.
What does FinSight do?
FinSight is your AI-powered financial advisor. It tracks your activity, habits, and goals to provide personalized suggestions and insights.
What is the Compare Search Box?
It’s a special search box that allows you to compare different banking and finance-related queries like charges, cards, fees, and interest rates.
How can I get help if I'm stuck?
You can ask questions in our Q&A forum or use the integrated chatbot for instant assistance.
